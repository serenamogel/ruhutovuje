    From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.

    Solgaleo (Japanese: ソルガレオGX SolgaleoGX) is a Metal-type Stage 2 Pokémon-GX card. It was first released as part of the Sun & Moon expansion.

    Card text

    Release information

    This card was included as a Regular card, a Full Art card, and as a Secret Rainbow Rare in the Sun & Moon expansion, first released in the Japanese Collection Sun. The Regular print features artwork by PLANETA, while the Full Art and Secret prints feature artwork by 5ban Graphics. A Jumbo version of the Regular print was included in the Alola Collection starting November 18, 2016. This print excluded the card's HP, Evolution Stage, and Attack Cost. A SM Black Star Promotional reprint featuring different artwork by 5ban Graphics was released in one of the two Legends of Alola Tins on March 17, 2017. This print was subsequently released in the Japanese GX Battle Boost subset. An entirely gold Full Art print with artwork by 5ban Graphics was included as a Secret card in the Ultra Prism expansion, first released in the Japanese GX Battle Boost subset. Another Jumbo version of the Regular print, this time with the card's HP, Evolution Stage, and Attack Cost, was available in the Solgaleo-GX Challenge Box released from June 7, 2019.
