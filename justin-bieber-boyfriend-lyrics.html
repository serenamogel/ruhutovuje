    “Boyfriend Lyrics” is a song by Justin Bieber, released in 2012 as the lead single from his third studio album “Believe”. The song was written by Bieber along with Mike Posner, Mason Levy, and Matthew Musto.

    It is an upbeat pop and R&B track that features Bieber showing off his smooth vocals and singing about wanting to be the perfect romantic partner for a girl he’s interested in. The song was well-received by fans and critics alike, and it debuted at number two on the US Billboard Hot 100 chart.

    The music video for “Boyfriend” features Bieber showcasing some of his signature dance moves, and he also shows off his cool and confident side as he pursues a beautiful woman in a variety of settings. The video has amassed over 900 million views on YouTube, making it one of Bieber’s most-viewed videos to date.

    About

    Justin Bieber

    Justin Bieber is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and actor born on March 1, 1994. He became popular in his early teens after being discovered on YouTube by talent manager Scooter Braun.

    He released his debut album “My World 2.0” in 2010 which was a commercial success and established him as a teenage heartthrob.

    As for his personal life, Justin Bieber has been in a high-profile relationship with American model Hailey Baldwin since 2018. The couple first dated briefly in 2015-2016 before rekindling their romance in 2018 and getting engaged that same year. They tied the knot in a private ceremony in September 2018 and have been happily married ever since.
