    History Of Crossword

    A writer from Liverpool named Arthur Wynne produced the first crossword puzzle that was ever published, and he is generally recognised as the creator of the well-known word game. It was published on December 21, 1913, in a Sunday edition of the New York World. In Pearson's Magazine in February 1922, a crossword made its debut in a British journal, and on February 1 of 1930, the Times published its first crossword. British crossword puzzles immediately established their own style and were significantly more challenging than those found in America.
