    Inside The Life Of American actor David Spade: Biography, Net Worth & more – David Spade, an American plays a role in the American movie industry as a top-tier actor.

    The 53-year-old is also a stand-up comedian, writer, and television host. He is widely known for playing the role of a writer and cast member in the NBC sketch comedy series Saturday Night Live from 1990 to 1996. 

    Apart from that he has made appearances in films series like Tommy Boy (1995), Black Sheep (1996), Senseless (1998), Joe Dirt (2001), Dickie Roberts: Former Child Star (2003), The Benchwarmers (2006), Grown Ups (2010). 

    Who is David Spade?

    On July 22, 1964, David Wayne Spade who is professionally known as David Spade was born in Birmingham, Michigan in the United States of America to Judith M. Spade, a writer and editor, and Wayne M. Spade, a sales rep as the couple’s youngest child amongst their three children. He was raised in both Scottsdale from age four and Casa Grande, Arizona.

    He had his high school education at Saguaro High School and proceeded to Scottsdale Community College for his college education but later transferred to Arizona State University, where he studied business. Being a natural prankster, Spade was pushed immediately into stand-up comedy by friends and appeared in nightclubs and college campuses all over the country.

    How old is David Spade?

    David is Leo according to his birth sign. He was born on July 22, 1964 therefore he is 53 years old presently. 

    What is David Spade’s net worth?

    From his multiple careers as an actor, comedian, and television personality, Spade has accumulated an estimated net worth of $70 million. 

    What is David Spade’s height and weight?

    David has light brown hair and blue eyes as well as a slim figure. The actor stands at an average height of 5 feet 7 inches (1.70 m) and weighs 65 kg (143.5 lbs). 

    What is David Spade’s Nationality and Ethnicity?

    David is American and has multiple ethnicities of German, English, Irish, and Scottish. 

    What is David Spade’s profession?

    He was doing a stand-up set when he got discovered by a talent agent. He then appeared in “Police Academy 4” (1987). Spade joined “Saturday Night Live” in 1990. He started as a writer and then ascended to a cast member. He made sketches for the show, which became hits. In the 1995/1996 season, Spade received a segment called “Spade in America” on the show.

    After a successful string of sketches and segments, Spade left “Saturday Night Live” in 1996. He began focusing on films some of which are Tommy Boy (1995), Black Sheep (1996), Senseless (1998), Joe Dirt (2001), Dickie Roberts: Former Child Star (2003), The Benchwarmers (2006), Grown Ups (2010), and the sequel “Grown Ups 2” (2013), and Warning Shot” (2018). 

    He has voiced video game characters, starred in series and sitcoms, and hosted celebrity roasts. For his good works he has received several accolades like two Golden Globe nominations, a Hollywood Walk of Fame star in 2003, and an Emmy nomination. 

    Are Kate Spade and David Spade related?

    Yes. The two celebrities are related. David Spade’s older brother, Andy Spade was once married to Kate Spade. The couple tied the knot in 1994. Unfortunately, they separated and moved on with their lives till the demise of the fashion designer Kate a few months later. 

    How long did David Spade and Jillian Grace date?

    Spade has been in numerous romantic acquaintances with celebrities and actresses like Heather Locklear, Lara Flynn Boyle, Julie Bowen, Teri Hatcher, and Naya Rivera. 

    He also dated former Playboy Playmate Jillian Grace.  Jilian and Spade started their romantic relationship in 2007, it however lasted for only a few months. 

    Does David Spade have kids?

    Yes. Even though David’s relationship with Jillian was a short one, they became parents to their only child and daughter, Harper, on August 26, 2008.
