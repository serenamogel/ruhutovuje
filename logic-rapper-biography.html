    Logic: Bio, Age, Parents, Siblings, Family, Education

    Logic was born on 22 January 1990 in Rockville, Maryland, U.S. As of 2024, his age is 33 and his real name is Sir Robert Bryson Hall II. He is also known as Psychological, Young Sinatra, Bobby Tarantino, and Doctor Destruction.

    He holds American nationality and is of mixed (African-American) descent. According to an astrologer chart, his zodiac sign is Aquarius.

    Regarding his parents’ details, his father’s name is Robert Bryson Hall and his mother’s name is missing at the moment. In his real name, his mother added Sir in his name. He grew up with his siblings, but his siblings were drug dealers and taught him how to produce crack cocaine.

    Logic: Professional Life, Career

    At the age of 14, he became interested in rap and hip-hop music after watching the Quentin Tarantino movie ‘Kill Bill: Volume 1’. He also met Solomon Taylor, who pushed him to create lyrics on instrumental tracks and quickly turned into a mentor.

    In 2009, he performed on the stage 2009, under the stage name ‘Psychological,’ where he started to perform opening acts for stars like Pitbull, EPMD, Method Man, Redman, and Ludacris. He officially released his first mixtape, ‘Young, Broke & Infamous’, over 250,000 times it was downloaded.

    In 2011, he released his second mixtape, ‘Young Sinatra’ which was a critical success and provided him with exposure to various publications. Many people have reviewed his studio album “Under Pressure,” the album reached number four on the ‘Billboard’ 200. The album achieved Gold status by the close of 2016 and sold more than 500,000 copies in the United States.
