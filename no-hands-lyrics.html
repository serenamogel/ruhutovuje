    No Hands is a popular hip-hop song by American rapper Waka Flocka Flame featuring Roscoe Dash and Wale.

    The song was released in 2010 as the third single from Waka Flocka Flame’s debut album “Flockaveli.

    It was a commercial success, reaching number 13 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart and being certified triple platinum by the RIAA.
