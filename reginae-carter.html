    Reginae Carter Biography

    Reginae Carter is best known as Rapper, Actress who has an estimated Net Worth of $2 Million. Hip-hop and R&B artist also known as Baby Carter. She is the daughter of famous rapper Lil Wayne.  She was born on November 29,1998, Louisiana. She was briefly a member of the R&B girl group OMG.  She was born when Lil Wayne and her mother Antonia Carter were in their teens.  She appeared alongside Tiffany Hines in the 2019 TV movie Pride & Prejudice: Atlanta.  She has four siblings: Cameron Carter, Reign Ryan Rushing, Dwayne Carter III, and Neal Carter. From 2018 to 2019, she dated YFN Lucci. Her zodiac sign is Sagittarius
