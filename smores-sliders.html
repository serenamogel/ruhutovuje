    S’mores Sliders

    Perfect for a crowd or just a quick treat – S’mores Waffle Sliders are kid-friendly and delicious!

    Course Appetizer Cuisine American Keyword s'mores sliders Prep Time 5 minutes minutes Cook Time 10 minutes minutes Total Time 15 minutes minutes Servings 4 Calories 388kcal Author Trish - Mom On Timeout

    Ingredients

    • 8 Eggos Homestyle Minis waffles
    • 1 oz Chocolate CandiQuik or Chocolate flavored candy coating
    • 4 Jumbo marshmallows
    • 4 Hershey's snack size chocolate bars

    Instructions

    • Prepare mini waffles according to package instructions and let cool.

    • Melt candy coating according to package instructions and dip waffles in chocolate. Let harden.

    • Place marshmallows on a baking sheet under broiler for 3-4 minutes. Watch carefully and remove when golden.

    • Break Hershey's in half and place on one mini waffle. Top with roasted marshmallow and second waffle. Enjoy!
