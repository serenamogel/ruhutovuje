    07 Sep 2024, 00:00

    The best yerba mate will give you a boost of energy without tasting bitter or being packed with sugar. The balance of these three things can be hard to find. In an attempt to help all the tea drinkers and caffeine lovers out there, I drank an unbelievable amount of this bottled and canned tea to find the best yerba mate out there.

    If you aren’t familiar with yerba mate, it is a naturally caffeinated tea that can be served hot or cold, sweet or unsweet. Today, we’re specifically talking about the canned or bottled yerba mate that you can buy at the grocery store. And whether you like your tea with zero sugar or like a sweet treat, there’s a packaged yerba mate beverage for you.

    Best Unsweetened

    Trader Joe’s Organic Sparkling Yerba MateTrader Joe’s

    If you’re looking for the best yerba mate that has no sugar added, pick up this version at Trader Joe’s. The carbonated fizz is nice and helps offset the bitterness of the tea. Plus, it’s organic! I feel like this would be a great yerba mate to drink if you’re hungover or have your period. The caffeine will give you much-needed energy and the mild carbonation will help settle your stomach.

    Best Low Calorie

    Clean Cause Lemon LimeAmazon

    If you’re looking for a low-calorie, low-sugar yerba mate that still has some sweetness, Clean Cause is your best bet. Lemon Lime is their best flavor, and it’s definitely the one you should reach for when you’re thirsty. It’s sweet, a little tart, and light. Do I hate the name? Yes. “Clean” is a dumb thing to call a drink. It makes me feel like I’m drinking detergent. Do I like the taste, though? Absolutely!

    Best Carbonated

    Guayaki Organic Sparkling Cranberry PomegranateAmazon

    If you poured this Guayaki Organic Sparkling Cranberry Pomegranate Yerba Mate into a champagne flute, I would think it was a refreshing, homemade mocktail. It is so tasty. It is really carbonated and sweet. You could cheers with this at midnight on New Year’s Eve for a bump of energy before partying all night long. I don’t drink much, and this is a great substitute for a cocktail, in my personal opinion.

It’s a wonderful morning yerba mate because you could brush your teeth and then drink it without experiencing that awful, post-brush bitterness. There’s nothing worse than a big ol’ swig of coffee after you’ve brushed your teeth. This is not only the best mint yerba mate, but it is also the best breakfast yerba mate.
