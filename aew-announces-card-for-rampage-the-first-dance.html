    17 Mar 2024, 00:00

    AEW will present a huge episode of Rampage this week on TNT. The First Dance will take place in the United Center on Friday. Many fans, and the TNT network, are expecting CM Punk’s debut. If Punk doesn’t show up, then AEW is dropping ton of hints for no reason. Odds are fans are in for a huge show, and now we know what matches to expect.

    Click here for our complete coverage of AEW Dynamite from this week.

    The Young Bucks are set to defend the AEW Tag Team Titles in a cage match at All Out. They will face the winners of a tournament. Private Party and Jurassic Express will square off on Rampage with All Out as their goal.

    Jade Cargill will take on Kiera Hogan, who just made her AEW debut on Dark where she lost to Hikaru Shida. Also, Jon Moxley will take on Daniel Garcia in the main event of the night.

    All this and more is expected for AEW Rampage: The First Dance. We will have complete coverage right here at Ringside News. You can check out the card (so far) below:

    Private Party vs Jurassic Express – AEW Tag Team contender match – Winner takes on Young Bucks in cage match at All Out

    Jade Cargill vs Kiera Hogan

    Jon Moxley vs Daniel Garcia
