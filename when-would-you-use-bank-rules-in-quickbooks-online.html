    What is a bank rule?

    Bank rules are a set of criteria that you define and are applied to incoming bank transactions. Use them with bank accounts connected to a bank feed or when you import a statement directly or via Auto Entry.

    Can you copy bank rules in QuickBooks Online?

    Copy banking rules

    From the Rules page, click Edit at the end of the row of the rule you want to change and select Copy from the list.In the form that appears, QuickBooks automatically adds “Copy of” to the existing rule name. Make the changes you want and click Save.
