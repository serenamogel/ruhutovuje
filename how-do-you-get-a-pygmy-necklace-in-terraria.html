    What are stingers used for in Terraria?

    Stingers are crafting materials which have a 2/3 (66.67%) / 1/1 (100%) chance to drop from Hornets and Spiked Jungle Slimes found in the Underground Jungle biome (Cavern). Stingers are used in most Jungle-themed crafting recipes. Excluding the consumable items, crafting all items requires 55 / 33 / 42 Stingers.

    How do you spawn a shark in Terraria?

    Sharks can only spawn within 250 tiles of the map's edge, or from a Shark Statue. Sharks do not flop around on land like most other fish, making them less dangerous to approach when beached. Along with other water enemies, Sharks will ignore players who aren't submerged in at least one tile of water.

    What happens when the Queen Bee dies?

    When a queen bee dies suddenly the colony is upset but acts quickly to rear a new one. Usually, the workers find eggs or larvae less than three days old and house them in specially constructed, vertically-hanging "queen cells." The fertilized eggs take about three days to hatch.
