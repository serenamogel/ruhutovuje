    The Bella Twins 

    The Bella Twins were featured in this week’s new episode of truTV’s new prank show Fameless, setting up a big surprise prank for an unsuspecting participant who thinks she’s helping set it up, when she’s actually the one being pranked by everyone else. 

    Nikki and Brie take part in a wrestling themed prank where an unsuspecting host thinks she is helping with a wrestling Hall of Fame induction, but she’s actually the one being set up after a surprise goes horribly wrong. The following clip aired on tonight’s show; you can watch the Bellas’ clip and read the show and episode synopsis below. 

    Fameless airs Mondays at 10pm ET/PT on truTV.

    From the mischievous mind of comedian and actor David Spade comes Fameless, a genre-busting prank show about how far real people will go for a moment in the spotlight. Each week, the series features a group of unsuspecting desperate-to-be-famous individuals who believe they’ve been cast on a reality show. However, unbeknownst to them, they are really filming a parody with improv actors creating over-the-top scenarios that steadily increase the absurdity and ridiculousness of each situation – testing the patience of everyone NOT in on the joke.

     

    : An aspiring cop’s chance to be a crime fighter crumbles because of his half-baked excuse for a partner, an MC’s hopes of surprising a famous pair of wrestling twins goes up in flames, and a would-be pranksters attempt at tricking a zookeeper turns grizzly.

    Cody Rhodes 

    Cody Rhodes will face Sonjay Dutt for the Global Force Wrestling Nex-Gen Championship at this year’s WrestleCade Showcase of Champions in Winston-Salem, North Carolina this November.

    Rhodes announced the title match earlier today on his Twitter account. The 2nd annual Showcase of Champions is part of WrestleCade V weekend and will take place at the Benton Convention Center on November 25-26. Rhodes will be making his only appearance on the 25th. 

    On a related note, Rhodes also mentioned his new theme song, “Kingdom” by Downstait, is now available on iTunes for purchase.
